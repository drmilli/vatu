About VATU VULA

The name Vatu Vula means White Rock – symbolising strength, purity, and stability. Inspired by our Christian faith, this project was envisioned to be more than just housing. It is a statement of excellence, reflecting values of strength, legacy, and peace.

Our homes are crafted to international embassy specifications, offering a standard of living that welcomes global residents with grace and security.
